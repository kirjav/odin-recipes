Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a classic Italian dish made with wide, flat pasta sheets layered with a meat sauce (ragù), béchamel sauce, cheese (like ricotta, mozzarella, and Parmesan), and seasonings, then baked in the oven until bubbly and golden brown, typically cut into square or rectangular servings; essentially, it's a layered pasta casserole with various fillings between each noodle sheet.
